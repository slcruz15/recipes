Lemon Chicken
-------------

2 T margarine
Flour
1/3 c white wine
1 can artichoke hearts
boned and skinned chicken breasts
1/2 c chicken broth
juice of 1 whole lemon

Flour chicken, melt margarine in skillet, brown chicken.  Mix remaining ingredients together and pour over chicken. Cook until liquid is reduced and thickened.  Serve with buttered noodles or rice.
